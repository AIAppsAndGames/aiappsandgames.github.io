Description

Establish life-changing habits and monitor daily targets with an offline-first, Material 3 interface. Track goal streaks, configure smart reminders, and log achievements with clean visual charts.

Key Features

  • • Interactive Charts: Beautiful custom progress tracking drawn with Jetpack Compose Canvas.
    • \n
  • • Habit Streaks: Count consecutive active days to earn rewards and keep up motivation.
    • \n
  • • Glance Widgets: View lists and check off tasks directly from your Android home screen.

Android Permissions & Data Safety

Required Permissions

  • • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Re-registers your daily reminder timers when device restarts.
    • \n
  • • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Delivers daily goal alarms at your chosen times.

Data Safety

Your tasks and habits are personal. All logs are cached locally using Room SQL. No external backups or accounts are required.
