Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy details how we handle information for the Android application AIVids - 4k AI Video Player, developed by AI Apps & Games.

2. Data Disclosures

All playback operations happen locally. AIVids does not read, upload, or index your private media files online. We do not monitor user behaviors, telemetry details, or location records.

3. System Permissions Usage

The app requests device permissions strictly to run core utility features. These requests are not used for tracking or backgrounds marketing purposes.

4. Security

Data stored locally utilizes Android filesystem sandboxing to prevent unauthorized local queries from third-party apps.

5. Contacts

For support or data deletion logs, email us at contact@aiappsandgames.com.
