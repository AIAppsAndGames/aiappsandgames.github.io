Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026

1. Overview

AI Apps & Games ("we", "our", or "us") builds high-quality native Android applications, including AI tools, productivity utilities, games, and calculators. This Privacy Policy explains our strategies regarding the compilation, usage, and safety of user records on our applications hosted on the Google Play Store.

2. Information Collection & Use

Most of our utility tools (e.g., Challenge Tracker, Finance Calculator, Bhagavad Gita Summary) are engineered as offline-first apps. They process data locally using Room SQLite wrappers and do not transmit personal files to any cloud server.

For cloud-powered integrations (e.g., Prompt to Pic, AI Vids), we securely transmit user-input prompt text to AI APIs (such as Google Gemini APIs) to compile assets. No device metrics, contact registries, or location records are read or transmitted.

3. Device Permissions

Our apps request access permissions (like RECORD_AUDIO for transcription or NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES for peer-to-peer file transfer) strictly to support active user features. These permissions are never used for silent tracking or telemetry.

4. Security of Data

All database files saved locally are kept securely within standard Android private storage limits. We advise keeping your smartphone OS updated to maintain local file encryption standards.

5. Data Deletion Requests

Since we do not compile accounts on central databases, data is removed automatically when you uninstall our application. For manual support, submit a query via our Support Page.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us via email at kishorrramani@gmail.com.
